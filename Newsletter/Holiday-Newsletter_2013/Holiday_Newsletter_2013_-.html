Happy Holidays!

 

Dear friends, another year has gone by and although I visited some of you and kept in touch with others, there were not enough opportunities to catch up with all of you. I wish for a chance to meet you again in 2014. In the meantime, this is an account of the things Fiona and I did throughout the year.
